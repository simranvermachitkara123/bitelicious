Veggie Lasagna

“Layers of veggies, sauce, and melty goodness.”

Serves: 4–6

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Rest Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 9–12 lasagna sheets (no-boil or regular)
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta (or paneer crumble)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan (optional)
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt & pepper
  • Italian seasoning or dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F). Sauté garlic in oil; add zucchini, mushrooms, pepper; season and cook until just tender. Wilt spinach.
  2. Spread a thin layer of marinara in a baking dish. Layer sheets, ricotta, veggies, mozzarella; repeat. Top with marinara and cheese.
  3. Cover with foil and bake 25–30 mins; uncover and bake 10 mins until bubbly. Rest 10 mins before slicing.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make it vegan?
A: Use vegan ricotta and mozzarella; it works great.

Q: Soggy lasagna fix?
A: Don’t over-sauce and pre-sauté watery veggies.

Q: Freeze it?
A: Yes, cool completely, wrap, and freeze up to 2 months.
