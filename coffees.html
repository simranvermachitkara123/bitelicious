☕ Coffees

Hot & cold coffees to energize your day!

Black Coffee

Ingredients

Ground coffee or instant coffee, Hot water, Sugar (optional)

Method

  • Brew coffee with hot water (or dissolve instant coffee).
  • Sweeten if desired.
  • Serve hot.

Iced Coffee

Ingredients

Strong brewed coffee (cooled), Milk (optional), Sugar, Ice

Method

  • Combine cooled coffee with sugar until dissolved.
  • Add milk if you like; pour over ice.
  • Serve chilled.

Cold Coffee

Ingredients

Chilled milk, Instant coffee, Sugar, Ice, Ice cream (optional)

Method

  • Blend milk, instant coffee, sugar, and ice until frothy.
  • Add a scoop of ice cream for a thicker shake (optional).
  • Serve immediately.

Cappuccino

Ingredients

Espresso, Hot milk, Milk foam, Sugar (optional)

Method

  • Brew a shot of espresso.
  • Froth milk to create foam.
  • Pour espresso into cup, add hot milk and top with foam.
  • Dust with cocoa (optional).

Espresso

Ingredients

Finely ground coffee, Hot water (espresso machine)

Method

  • Pull a 25–30 ml shot using an espresso machine.
  • Serve immediately in a warm cup.

Mocha

Ingredients

Espresso, Hot milk, Cocoa powder or chocolate syrup, Sugar (optional)

Method

  • Stir cocoa/syrup with a little hot milk to dissolve.
  • Add espresso and more hot milk; sweeten to taste.
  • Top with foam or whipped cream (optional).

Latte

Ingredients

Espresso, Steamed milk, Thin layer of foam

Method

  • Pull a shot of espresso into a cup.
  • Steam milk and pour slowly into espresso.
  • Finish with a thin layer of foam.
