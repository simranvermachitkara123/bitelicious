Vegetarian Tacos

“Colorful, Fresh, and Bursting with Flavor – Perfect Veggie Tacos!”

Serves: 4

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cooking Time: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup black beans (cooked)
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • Fresh cilantro & lime wedges
  • Salt, pepper, and chili powder to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté bell peppers and corn with spices until tender.
  2. Warm the tortillas on a skillet.
  3. Layer black beans, sautéed veggies, and avocado slices on tortillas.
  4. Top with cilantro and a squeeze of lime.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!

FAQs:

Q: Can I use flour tortillas?
A: Yes, but corn tortillas give a more authentic taste.

Q: Can I add cheese?
A: Absolutely, shredded cheddar or cotija cheese works well.
