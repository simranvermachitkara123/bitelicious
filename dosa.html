Serves: 4 people

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Rest Time: 8 hours (fermentation)

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups dosa rice (parboiled rice)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak urad dal with fenugreek seeds for 4-6 hours.
  2. Wash and soak dosa rice separately for 4-6 hours.
  3. Grind urad dal to a smooth and fluffy batter using water gradually.
  4. Grind rice coarsely and mix with the urad dal batter.
  5. Add salt and mix well. Cover and leave for fermentation 8 hours or overnight.
  6. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle and lightly grease with oil or ghee.
  7. Pour a ladle of batter and spread thinly in a circular motion.
  8. Drizzle oil or ghee around edges and cook until the dosa is golden and crispy.
  9. Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or potato filling.

FAQs:

Q: Can I use instant dosa mix?
A: Yes, but freshly ground and fermented batter gives the best flavor and crispiness.

Q: How do I make crispy dosa?
A: Spread the batter thinly and cook on medium-high heat with little oil.

Q: Can I make vegan dosa?
A: Yes, dosa is naturally vegan since it contains no dairy or eggs.

Q: How long can I store fermented batter?
A: Store in the fridge for up to 2 days. Stir gently before making dosas.
