Virgin Mojito

Ingredients

Fresh mint leaves, Lemon wedges, Sugar, Soda water, Ice cubes

Method

  • Add mint leaves, lemon wedges, and sugar to a sturdy glass.
  • Muddle gently to release mint oils and lemon juice.
  • Fill the glass with ice.
  • Top with chilled soda water and stir.
  • Garnish with mint sprig and lemon slice. Serve immediately.

Fruit Punch

Ingredients

Orange juice, Pineapple juice, Apple juice, Mixed berry juice, Lemon juice, Soda, Ice

Method

  • Combine all juices in a jug and mix.
  • Add lemon juice and plenty of ice.
  • Top with soda for fizz and stir once.
  • Serve with citrus slices and mint.

Aam Panna

Ingredients

Raw mango, Sugar or jaggery, Roasted cumin powder, Black salt, Mint, Water, Ice

Method

  • Boil raw mango until soft, cool and scoop pulp.
  • Blend pulp with sugar/jaggery, cumin, black salt, mint, and water.
  • Chill, then serve over ice. Adjust sweetness and salt to taste.

Watermelon Cooler

Ingredients

Watermelon chunks (seedless), Lemon juice, Sugar (optional), Mint, Ice

Method

  • Blend watermelon with lemon juice.
  • Sweeten if needed; strain if you prefer.
  • Pour over ice and garnish with mint.

Peach Cooler

Ingredients

Peach nectar or puree, Lemon juice, Sugar syrup, Soda, Ice

Method

  • Add peach nectar, lemon juice, and sugar syrup to a glass.
  • Fill with ice and top with soda.
  • Stir gently and serve with peach slice.

Rose Milk

Ingredients

Chilled milk, Rose syrup, Sugar (optional), Ice, Rose petals (optional)

Method

  • Mix rose syrup into chilled milk until uniform.
  • Add sugar if needed.
  • Serve over ice; garnish with rose petals.
