Guacamole

â€œCreamy, zesty, and fresh â€“ the ultimate Mexican dip.â€

Serves: 3-4 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Rest Time: None

Cooking Time: 0 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Scoop avocado flesh into a bowl and mash with a fork.
  2. Add lime juice, onion, tomato, and cilantro.
  3. Mix until combined but slightly chunky.
  4. Season with salt and serve with tortilla chips.

FAQs:

Q: How do I keep guacamole from browning?
A: Cover tightly with plastic wrap pressed against the surface or add extra lime juice.

Q: Can I make it ahead?
A: Yes, but best enjoyed fresh within 24 hours.
