Serves: 3

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cooking Time: 10 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup onions (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salsa & sour cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. SautÃ© bell peppers, mushrooms, and onions until soft.
  2. Place a tortilla on a skillet, sprinkle cheese and veggies.
  3. Top with another tortilla and cook until golden brown.
  4. Flip and cook the other side.
  5. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa & sour cream.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make it vegan?
A: Yes, use vegan cheese and tortillas.

Q: Can I add beans?
A: Yes, black beans or pinto beans make it heartier.
