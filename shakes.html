Creamy & delicious shakes for your sweet tooth!

Banana Shake

Ingredients

Ripe banana, Chilled milk, Sugar or honey, Ice, A pinch of cardamom (optional)

Method

  • Blend banana, milk, and sugar/honey until smooth.
  • Add ice and blend again for a frothy finish.
  • Sprinkle cardamom if using; serve chilled.

Mango Shake

Ingredients

Ripe mango, Chilled milk, Sugar, Ice

Method

  • Blend chopped mango with milk and sugar.
  • Add ice and blend until creamy.
  • Serve immediately.

Chocolate Shake

Ingredients

Chilled milk, Cocoa powder or chocolate syrup, Sugar, Ice cream (optional), Ice

Method

  • Blend milk, cocoa/syrup, and sugar until combined.
  • Add a scoop of ice cream (optional) and ice; blend frothy.
  • Serve topped with chocolate shavings.

Strawberry Shake

Ingredients

Fresh strawberries, Chilled milk, Sugar/honey, Ice

Method

  • Blend strawberries with milk and sugar/honey.
  • Add ice and blend smooth.
  • Serve with a sliced strawberry on top.

Oreo Shake

Ingredients

Oreo biscuits, Chilled milk, Vanilla ice cream, Sugar (optional), Ice

Method

  • Crush Oreos roughly; add to blender with milk and ice cream.
  • Blend until thick and creamy.
  • Top with crushed Oreos and serve immediately.

Vanilla Shake

Ingredients

Chilled milk, Vanilla ice cream, Vanilla essence, Sugar (optional), Ice

Method

  • Blend milk, ice cream, and a few drops of vanilla essence.
  • Adjust sweetness; add ice and blend frothy.
  • Serve with whipped cream (optional).
