Mexican Street Corn (Elote)

“Grilled corn smothered with creamy, cheesy, and tangy flavors.”

Serves: 4 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Rest Time: None

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears corn (grilled)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup cotija cheese (crumbled)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Grill corn until slightly charred.
  2. Mix mayonnaise and sour cream in a bowl.
  3. Spread mixture over hot corn.
  4. Sprinkle with cotija cheese, chili powder, and cilantro.
  5. Serve with lime wedges.

FAQs:

Q: Can I use frozen corn?
A: Fresh corn is best, but frozen works if thawed and grilled properly.

Q: What if I don’t have cotija cheese?
A: Substitute with feta or parmesan.
