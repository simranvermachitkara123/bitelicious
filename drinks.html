Mango Smoothie

Ingredients: Mango, Yogurt, Honey, Ice cubes

Method: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Serve chilled.

Lemon Iced Tea

Ingredients: Tea, Lemon juice, Sugar, Ice

Method: Brew tea, add lemon juice & sugar, pour over ice.

Cold Coffee

Ingredients: Coffee, Milk, Sugar, Ice

Method: Blend coffee, milk & sugar with ice. Serve chilled.

Banana Milkshake

Ingredients: Banana, Milk, Honey

Method: Blend banana, milk & honey. Serve chilled.

Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredients: Strawberries, Yogurt, Honey

Method: Blend strawberries with yogurt & honey.

Mojito

Ingredients: Mint, Lemon, Soda, Sugar

Method: Muddle mint & lemon, add soda & sugar. Serve with ice.

Hot Chocolate

Ingredients: Milk, Cocoa powder, Sugar, Cream

Method: Heat milk with cocoa & sugar, top with cream.

Green Smoothie

Ingredients: Spinach, Banana, Apple, Honey

Method: Blend spinach, banana & apple with honey.

Aam Panna

Ingredients: Raw mangoes, Mint leaves, Cumin powder, Black salt, Sugar, Cold water

Method: Boil raw mangoes, peel & extract pulp. Blend with mint, cumin, black salt & sugar. Mix with cold water and serve chilled.

Thandai

Ingredients: Milk, Almonds, Saffron, Fennel seeds, Cardamom, Sugar

Method: Grind almonds, fennel & cardamom into paste. Mix into chilled milk with saffron & sugar. Serve cold.

Jamun Cooler

Ingredients: Jamun fruit, Lemon juice, Black salt, Sugar, Ice

Method: Blend jamun pulp with lemon juice, black salt & sugar. Serve over ice.

Hibiscus Iced Tea

Ingredients: Dried hibiscus petals, Water, Honey, Lemon juice, Ice

Method: Brew hibiscus petals in hot water, strain & chill. Add honey & lemon. Serve over ice.

Chaas (Masala Buttermilk)

Ingredients: Yogurt, Water, Roasted cumin, Salt, Mint leaves

Method: Whisk yogurt with water. Add roasted cumin, salt & crushed mint. Serve chilled.

Strawberry Basil Cooler

Ingredients: Fresh strawberries, Basil leaves, Lemon juice, Sugar, Soda water

Method: Muddle strawberries & basil. Add lemon juice & sugar. Top with soda water and serve chilled.

Masala Chai

Ingredients: Tea, Spices, Milk, Sugar

Method: Boil tea with spices, add milk & sugar.

Herbal Tea

Ingredients: Herbal leaves, Honey

Method: Steep herbal leaves in hot water, sweeten with honey.
