Mango Smoothie

Ingredients

Ripe mango, Yogurt (or milk), Honey or sugar, Ice cubes

Method

  • Peel and chop the ripe mango into small pieces.
  • Add mango, yogurt (or milk), and honey/sugar to a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Add ice cubes and blend again for a chilled smoothie.
  • Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

Cold Coffee

Ingredients

Coffee, Milk, Sugar, Ice

Method

  • Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool.
  • Add cooled coffee, milk, sugar, and ice to a blender.
  • Blend until frothy and smooth.
  • Pour into a tall glass and serve chilled.

Mojito

Ingredients

Fresh mint leaves, Lime, Sugar, Soda water, Ice cubes

Method

  • Muddle fresh mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in a glass.
  • Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour in soda water and stir gently.
  • Garnish with extra mint leaves and lime slices.
  • Serve chilled and refreshing.

Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

Cocoa powder, Milk, Sugar, Whipped cream (optional)

Method

  • Heat milk in a saucepan over medium flame.
  • Whisk in cocoa powder and sugar until smooth.
  • Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  • Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream if desired.
  • Serve hot and cozy.

Aam Panna

Ingredients

Raw mangoes, Sugar, Black salt, Roasted cumin powder, Mint

Method

  • Boil raw mangoes until soft, then peel and extract pulp.
  • Blend pulp with sugar, black salt, and cumin powder.
  • Mix with chilled water.
  • Garnish with mint leaves and serve cold.

Thandai

Ingredients

Milk, Almonds, Cashews, Fennel seeds, Cardamom, Black pepper, Sugar, Rose water

Method

  • Soak nuts and spices, then grind into a smooth paste.
  • Mix paste with chilled milk and sugar.
  • Add rose water for fragrance.
  • Serve cold, garnished with saffron strands.
