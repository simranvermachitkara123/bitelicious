Pongal

â€œCreamy, Comforting, and Flavored with Black Pepper â€“ Classic South Indian Pongal.â€

Serves: 3-4 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Rest Time: None

Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (raw rice or sona masoori)
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Cashew nuts (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Wash rice and moong dal thoroughly.
  2. In a pan or pressure cooker, add rice, moong dal, and water. Cook until soft and mushy.
  3. In a small pan, heat ghee. Add cumin seeds, crushed black pepper, grated ginger, curry leaves, and cashew nuts. Fry until aromatic and cashews are golden.
  4. Pour this tempering over the cooked rice and dal mixture. Mix well.
  5. Adjust salt to taste and cook for another 2-3 minutes on low heat.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
