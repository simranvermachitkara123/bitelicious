Adrak Chai

Ingredients

Water, Milk, Black tea leaves, Fresh ginger, Sugar

Method

  • Boil water with crushed ginger to release flavor.
  • Add tea leaves; simmer 1 minute.
  • Add milk; boil, sweeten, and strain.
