Orange Juice

Ingredients

Fresh oranges, Sugar (optional), Ice

Method

  • Juice the oranges; strain if preferred.
  • Sweeten lightly if needed.
  • Serve chilled over ice.

Apple Juice

Ingredients

Apples, Lemon juice, Sugar (optional), Water, Ice

Method

  • Blend chopped apples with a little water.
  • Strain, add lemon juice to prevent browning.
  • Sweeten if required; serve over ice.

Pineapple Juice

Ingredients

Fresh pineapple chunks, Sugar (optional), Water, Ice

Method

  • Blend pineapple with a splash of water until smooth.
  • Strain (optional) and adjust sweetness.
  • Serve chilled over ice.

Watermelon Juice

Ingredients

Seedless watermelon, Lemon juice, Sugar (optional), Mint, Ice

Method

  • Blend watermelon until smooth; strain if you like.
  • Add lemon juice and sweeten if needed.
  • Pour over ice and garnish with mint.

Grape Juice

Ingredients

Seedless grapes, Sugar (optional), Water, Lemon juice (optional), Ice

Method

  • Blend grapes with a little water; strain.
  • Add sugar and a splash of lemon juice if desired.
  • Serve chilled.

Carrot Juice

Ingredients

Carrots, Ginger (optional), Lemon juice, Water, Honey (optional), Ice

Method

  • Blend chopped carrots with water (and ginger if using).
  • Strain; add lemon juice and honey to taste.
  • Serve over ice.

Mixed Fruit Juice

Ingredients

Orange, Apple, Pineapple, Grapes, Lemon juice, Sugar (optional), Ice

Method

  • Blend all chopped fruits together; add a little water if needed.
  • Strain if you prefer a smoother texture.
  • Finish with lemon juice; serve chilled.
