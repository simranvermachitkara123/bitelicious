🍰 Dessert Recipes

A collection of 15 delicious treats you can try at home!

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Flour, Cocoa powder, Eggs, Sugar, Butter

Method

  • Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  • Combine and pour into a baking tin.
  • Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  • Cool and serve with icing.

Cheesecake

Ingredients

Cream cheese, Sugar, Eggs, Graham crackers, Butter

Method

  • Prepare crust with crushed crackers and butter.
  • Mix cream cheese, sugar, and eggs.
  • Pour filling into crust and bake at 160°C for 45 minutes.
  • Chill before serving.

Apple Pie

Ingredients

Apples, Sugar, Cinnamon, Pie crust, Butter

Method

  • Slice apples and mix with sugar & cinnamon.
  • Fill pie crust with mixture.
  • Top with second crust and bake at 190°C for 50 minutes.

Brownies

Ingredients

Chocolate, Cocoa powder, Sugar, Eggs, Flour

Method

  • Melt chocolate and butter together.
  • Mix with sugar and eggs.
  • Fold in flour and cocoa.
  • Bake at 180°C for 25 minutes.

Tiramisu

Ingredients

Mascarpone cheese, Coffee, Ladyfingers, Cocoa powder, Eggs

Method

  • Dip ladyfingers in coffee.
  • Layer with mascarpone mixture.
  • Repeat and dust with cocoa powder.
  • Refrigerate before serving.

Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients

Flour, Sugar, Eggs, Butter, Vanilla extract

Method

  • Prepare cupcake batter with all ingredients.
  • Fill liners 2/3 full.
  • Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

Dark chocolate, Eggs, Sugar, Cream

Method

  • Melt chocolate and let cool.
  • Whip cream and fold into chocolate.
  • Add whipped egg whites.
  • Chill before serving.

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

Milk, Cream, Sugar, Vanilla

Method

  • Heat milk, cream, sugar, and vanilla.
  • Cool and churn in an ice cream maker.
  • Freeze until firm.

Fruit Tart

Ingredients

Tart crust, Pastry cream, Fresh fruits

Method

  • Fill baked crust with pastry cream.
  • Top with fresh fruits.
  • Glaze lightly and chill.

Panna Cotta

Ingredients

Cream, Sugar, Gelatin, Vanilla

Method

  • Heat cream and sugar.
  • Add gelatin and stir until dissolved.
  • Pour into molds and chill.

Donuts

Ingredients

Flour, Sugar, Yeast, Milk, Oil

Method

  • Prepare yeast dough and let rise.
  • Shape into rings and fry.
  • Glaze with sugar icing.

Macarons

Ingredients

Almond flour, Egg whites, Sugar, Food coloring

Method

  • Whip egg whites with sugar.
  • Fold into almond flour mixture.
  • Pipe and bake at 150°C.
  • Fill with buttercream.

Pavlova

Ingredients

Egg whites, Sugar, Cream, Fresh fruits

Method

  • Whip egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks.
  • Bake meringue at low heat.
  • Top with cream and fruits.

Banana Bread

Ingredients

Bananas, Flour, Sugar, Butter, Eggs

Method

  • Mash bananas and mix with wet ingredients.
  • Add dry ingredients and fold.
  • Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes.

Churros

Ingredients

Flour, Butter, Water, Sugar, Cinnamon

Method

  • Boil butter and water, add flour to form dough.
  • Pipe dough into hot oil and fry.
  • Roll in cinnamon sugar.
