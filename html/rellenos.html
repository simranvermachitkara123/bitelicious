Chiles Rellenos

“Cheesy, Spicy, and Flavorful – A Mexican Classic!”

Serves: 4

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cooking Time: 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 poblano peppers
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (queso fresco or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 3 eggs (separated)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Oil for frying
  • Tomato sauce for serving

Instructions:

  1. Roast peppers until skin blisters. Peel skin carefully.
  2. Make a slit and stuff with cheese.
  3. Coat in flour, then dip in whipped egg batter.
  4. Fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve with warm tomato sauce.

FAQs:

Q: Can I bake instead of fry?
A: Yes, baking is a healthier option.

Q: What cheese works best?
A: Queso fresco, mozzarella, or Monterey Jack.
