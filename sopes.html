â€œThick corn cakes topped with beans, veggies, and cheese.â€

Serves: 4 people

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Rest Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups masa harina (corn flour)
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • Salsa for topping

Instructions:

  1. Mix masa harina, water, and salt into dough.
  2. Divide into balls, flatten, and cook on skillet until golden.
  3. Pinch edges to form raised borders.
  4. Top with beans, lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
