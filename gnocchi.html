Gnocchi

“Pillow-soft potato dumplings.”

Serves: 3–4

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Rest Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 600 g starchy potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (approx., plus extra for dusting)
  • 1 egg (optional; skip for egg-free and add a bit more flour)
  • Salt
  • Butter or olive oil, for finishing
  • Sage leaves or pesto/tomato sauce (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Boil potatoes with skin until tender; peel while warm and mash or rice until smooth.
  2. Mix with salt, egg (if using), and enough flour to form a soft, slightly tacky dough.
  3. Divide into ropes and cut into bite-sized pieces; roll on a fork for ridges if desired.
  4. Boil in salted water until they float (1–2 mins). Toss with butter & sage or your favorite sauce.

FAQs:

Q: Dough is sticky?
A: Dust with more flour but don’t overwork to keep them tender.

Q: Can I freeze them?
A: Yes, freeze on a tray, then bag; cook from frozen.

Q: Sauce ideas?
A: Browned butter & sage, pesto, or a light tomato sauce.
