Pesto Pasta

“Fresh basil magic in every bite.”

Serves: 2–3

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Rest Time:not required

Cooking Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 250 g pasta (fusilli/penne/spaghetti)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts or walnuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1–2 tbsp pasta cooking water
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional), halved

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve water and drain.
  2. Blend basil, nuts, garlic, parmesan (if using), and oil to a coarse paste. Season.
  3. Toss pasta with pesto, loosening with pasta water. Add tomatoes if you like and serve.

FAQs:

Q: Nut-free option?
A: Use sunflower seeds or skip nuts entirely.

Q: Store pesto?
A: Refrigerate 3–4 days topped with a thin oil layer.

Q: Make it vegan?
A: Use nutritional yeast instead of parmesan.
