Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

“Minimal ingredients. Maximum flavor.”

Serves: 2

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Rest Time:5 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4–5 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1–2 dried red chilies or 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (2 tbsp)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly cracked black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp pasta cooking water (as needed)
  • Grated parmesan (optional, for serving)
  • Lemon zest (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water and drain.
  2. Warm olive oil in a pan over low heat. Add garlic and chili; gently sauté until fragrant and lightly golden (do not burn).
  3. Toss in the cooked spaghetti with a splash of pasta water. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stir in parsley (and lemon zest if using). Finish with parmesan if desired and serve immediately.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make it without chili?
A: Yes, skip chili for a milder version.

Q: Which oil is best?
A: Extra virgin olive oil gives the best flavor.

Q: How to prevent bitter garlic?
A: Keep heat low and remove garlic once lightly golden.
