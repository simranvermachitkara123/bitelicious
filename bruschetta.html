Bruschetta

“Toasty bread. Juicy tomatoes. Fresh basil.”

Serves: 4

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Rest Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 3 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves (1 minced for topping, 1 whole for rubbing)
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Mix tomatoes, onion, minced garlic, basil, olive oil, balsamic, salt & pepper. Rest 10 mins.
  2. Toast baguette slices. Rub with cut garlic while warm.
  3. Spoon tomato mixture over toasts and serve immediately.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make the topping ahead?
A: Yes, 4–6 hours chilled; add basil just before serving.

Q: Bread alternatives?
A: Sourdough or ciabatta works well.

Q: Keep toast crisp?
A: Top just before serving or serve topping on the side.
